> [!definition|*] Row Partition of a Matrix
> 
> Let a matrix $A \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}$. Then we can define $A$ using a **row partition** as
> $$
> A = \begin{bmatrix}
> A(1,:) \\
> \hdashline 
> A(2,:) \\
> \hdashline 
> \vdots  \\
> \hdashline A(m,:)
> \end{bmatrix}_{m \times n}
> $$

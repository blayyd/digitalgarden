> [!theorem|1] Classic Subset Results
> 
> Let $A$, $B$, and $C$ be any sets. Then
> 1) $\emptyset \subseteq A$.
> 2) $A \subseteq A$
> 3) If $A \subseteq B$ and $B \subseteq C$, then $A \subseteq C$.

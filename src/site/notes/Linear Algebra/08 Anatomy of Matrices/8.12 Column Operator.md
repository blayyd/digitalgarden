> [!definition|*] Column Operator
> 
> Let $A \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}$. Let $k \in [n]$ ($k$ is an element of the set of columns). Then, we define the **column operator**
> $$
> \text{Column}_{k}(A) = A(:,k) = \begin{bmatrix}
> a_{1k} \\
> a_{2k} \\
> \vdots \\
> a_{mk}
> \end{bmatrix}_{m \times 1}
> $$
